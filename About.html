About Us

Crispy & Chips

Crispy & Chips, which started as a part-time business in 2024-2025 with an initial capital of only RM100, has grown into a successful snack business. Since 2017, we have continued to explore and introduce various high-quality chip variants through our online platform.

Our experience of being invited to share on the radio and at colleges reflects our dedication to sharing knowledge and inspiration. Our website covers the history of our business journey, our experiences on the radio, as well as an overview of the office snacks that became the foundation of our success. With a focus on innovation and quality, Crispy & Chips continues to provide a satisfying snack experience for our loyal customers.
